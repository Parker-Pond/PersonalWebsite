Things About Me

My name is Parker, and I am from Las Vegas. I married in 2018 to my beautiful wife Julie. She is a special ed pre-school teacher in a school near our house.

I studied psychology, entrepreneurship, and business in college but never felt satisfied with my options. I decided to open a restaurant, so I started running them as an assistant manager to learn the ropes. Having lost every weekend for the last few years I decided that opening a restaurant was not for me.

I remembers my love for web development in high school and decided to try my hand at it. I fell back in love with it and decided to take a more structured and guided path via a Bootcamp.

My local college partnered with Fullstack Academy, so I decided to trust my beloved school. I have been loving the program and look forward to getting into the field.
